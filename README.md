👋 About Me
- Clinical Psychologist and areseaecher
- I study the **dynamic psychosocial mechanisms** that promote flourishing and use them to create **mechanism-based treatment**. A growing focus of my research is how these patterns operate differently in **socially anxious adolescents with ADHD** — and what that demands of intervention design.

⭐ Highlights
- 🇬🇧 Supervised the **first randomised controlled trial designed to evaluate the efficacy of a digital single-session intervention (SSI)** for young people in the UK — [JCPP Advances (2025)](https://acamh.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jcv2.70026)
- 📊 Authored a **full meta-analytic review of self-administered single-session interventions** — [Journal of Child Psychology and Psychiatry (2026)](https://acamh.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/jcpp.70192)
- 🧪 Built a **mechanism-based adapted intervention platform** for single-case experimental design (SCED) evaluation

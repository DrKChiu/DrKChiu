👋 About Me
- I'm passionate about advancing **digital mental health** through evidence-based practice and innovative research. My work bridges clinical psychology, digital mental health, and applied AI, with a focus on young people's wellbeing.

⭐ Highlights
- 🇬🇧 Led the **first randomised controlled trial designed to evaluate the efficacy of a digital single-session intervention (SSI)** for young people in the UK — [JCPP Advances (2025)](https://acamh.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jcv2.70026)
- 📊 Authored a **full meta-analytic review of self-administered single-session interventions** — [Journal of Child Psychology and Psychiatry (2025)](https://acamh.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/jcpp.70192)
- 🧪 Built a **mechanism-based adapted intervention platform** for single-case experimental design (SCED) evaluation

🌐 Digital Mental Health
- Designing and rigorously evaluating scalable, accessible digital interventions for young people
- Single-session interventions (SSIs) — bringing low-intensity, high-reach support to those who need it most
- Mechanism-based intervention design, grounded in the active ingredients of what makes therapy work

- Current Projects: AI, ecological momentary assessment (EMA), digital tools for real-time mental health monitoring
